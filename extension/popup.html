We estimate the power usage of the chrome (P) based on two data from your computer, the maximum cpu power (C) and the current cpu usage (X). Then we use this formula to estimate power usage:
P = (X * C) / (0.412 X + 0.246)
This numbers in the formula is derived from the average power consumption percentage of the cpu within an idle system and a fully loaded system. Data comes from the article "Power Consumption Breakdown on a Modern Laptop" by Aqeel Mahesri, and V. Vardhan (2005).
